Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This policy explains how ads.christine uses data when people interact with our Instagram comment and direct message automation.

Data We Process

When you comment on or send a message to ads.christine on Instagram, we may process your Instagram username, Instagram-scoped user ID, comment or message text, comment ID, message ID, media ID, timestamps, and automation delivery status.

How We Use Data

We use this data only to respond to your requested keyword, deliver the quiz link or free resource you asked for, prevent duplicate automated replies, troubleshoot delivery errors, and maintain account safety.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your Instagram message or comment data with advertisers or unrelated third parties. Data may be processed by Meta as part of Instagram's platform and by hosting or infrastructure providers used to run the automation.

Retention

Automation logs are kept only as long as needed for delivery, deduplication, troubleshooting, and compliance. We avoid storing full conversation history unless it is necessary to respond to your request.

Your Choices

You can stop interacting with the automation at any time. You may also request deletion of automation records related to your Instagram interaction by following our data deletion instructions.
